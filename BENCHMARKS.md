## Benchmarks

Static mirror of `docs/site/public/benchmark-data.json` for GitHub previews,
search, and LLM context. The interactive dashboard remains available at
<https://dhruv1794.github.io/mdcompress/>.

Numbers below are **bytes** (not tokens) across the 20-repo corpus defined
in `docs/benchmark-repos.json`. They are regenerated by the weekly
`Benchmark & Deploy Pages` workflow; if you suspect drift, trigger a
`workflow_dispatch` and pull `benchmark-data.json`.

For per-rule attribution see [`docs/RULES_IMPACT.md`](docs/RULES_IMPACT.md).
